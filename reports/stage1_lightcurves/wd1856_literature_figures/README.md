# WD 1856 literature source manifest

Third-party manuscripts and extracted figures are intentionally kept outside
the Git-managed repository. Local reference copies, when present, live under
`data_local/reference_material/wd1856_literature_figures/` and remain subject
to the original publishers' and authors' terms.

Authoritative sources:

- Vanderburg et al. (2020), *A Giant Planet Candidate Transiting a White
  Dwarf*, Nature: [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-2713-y) and
  [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.07282).
- Limbach et al. (2025), *Thermal Emission and Confirmation of the Frigid White
  Dwarf Exoplanet WD 1856+534b*: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.16982).

Download source material directly from these records when a local reference
copy is unavailable. Any presentation use should credit the original paper on
the slide and follow the applicable reuse terms.
